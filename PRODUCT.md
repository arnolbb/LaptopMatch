# LaptopMatch

## Register
product

## What it does
AI-powered laptop recommendation wizard for the Indonesian market. Users answer a short series of questions (budget, use case, priorities, brand/OS preference) and receive 3 curated laptop recommendations with specs, pros/cons, and buying advice — all generated by Gemini AI.

## Target users
Indonesian consumers (students, professionals, creators, gamers) who want personalized laptop recommendations without hours of research. Age 18-40, tech-curious but not necessarily tech-expert.

## Brand personality
Confident, editorial, minimal. Like a trusted tech journalist who knows the Indonesian laptop market inside-out. Not flashy, not corporate — authoritative yet approachable. The interface should feel like a premium editorial experience, not a SaaS dashboard or an AI chatbot.

## Anti-references
- Generic AI SaaS landing pages (gradient text, neon glow, sparkle icons, purple-blue gradients)
- Over-designed dashboards with unnecessary decorations
- Corporate bland interfaces
- ChatGPT-style chat interfaces for recommendations

## Strategic principles
1. Editorial elegance over SaaS aesthetics — this is a curated experience, not a tool
2. Content-first: the laptop recommendations ARE the product, design serves them
3. Indonesian market identity: prices in Rupiah, local marketplace references, Bahasa Indonesia throughout
4. Dark-first with refined contrast — sophisticated, not flashy
5. Purposeful motion: transitions serve navigation, never decorate
